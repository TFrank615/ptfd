Start Your Journey

Why Join Pleasant Township FD?

Serving as a firefighter or EMT is one of the most rewarding ways to give back to your community. At Pleasant Township, we are neighbors helping neighbors. Whether you are looking to start a career in emergency services or simply want to serve Catawba in your spare time, there is a place for you on our truck.

Free Training

We provide all necessary fire and EMS training at no cost to you. Gain state certifications and life-saving skills.

Brotherhood

Join a second family. The bonds formed in the fire service are unlike any other. We stand together, on and off duty.

Serve Your Community

Be the calm in someone's worst moment. Make a tangible difference in the safety of Catawba and Pleasant Township.

Basic Requirements

  • Must be at least 18 years of age
  • Valid Ohio Driver's License
  • High School Diploma or GED
  • Clean criminal background check
  • Pass a physical agility test
  • Reside within a reasonable response time

Training Expectations

Firefighting is physically and mentally demanding. We expect our members to attend weekly training drills and maintain their certifications.

Weekly Drills

Every Tuesday at 19:00 hours.

Certification

Ohio Volunteer Firefighter (36hr) minimum within 1 year.

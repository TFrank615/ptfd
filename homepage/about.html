ABOUT OUR DEPARTMENT

Proudly Serving Catawba and Pleasant Township since 1948

Our History

The Pleasant Township Fire Department was established in 1948 with a mission to provide dedicated fire protection and emergency services to our growing community. What started as a small group of concerned citizens has grown into a modern, capable force.

For over 75 years, we have been a pillar of safety for the Village of Catawba and the surrounding Pleasant Township area. Through decades of change, our commitment to "Honor, Duty, and Service" has remained unwavering.

Our Mission

To protect the lives and property of the citizens of Pleasant Township through professional emergency response, fire prevention, and community education.

Integrity

Community

Professionalism
